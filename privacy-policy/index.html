DreamLog Privacy Policy

"Five stars for your dreams."

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Short version: DreamLog does not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personal information. It has no accounts and no login. Your sleep logs are tied to an anonymous identifier stored only on your device.

What we store

  • An anonymous device ID. On first launch the app generates a random UUID (a string of random characters). It is not linked to your identity and is used only to associate your own sleep entries with your device.
  • Your sleep entries. The bedtime, wake time, quality rating, optional note, and computed duration for each night you log.
  • Your preferences. Sleep goal and reminder settings.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email address, or account credentials.
  • No location data.
  • No motion, microphone, or any device-sensor data.
  • No advertising identifiers and no third-party analytics or ad SDKs.

Notifications

If you enable bedtime or log reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. No push server is involved and no data leaves your device for this feature.

Where your data lives

Sleep entries are stored on your device and on the DreamLog backend solely to sync between sessions, keyed only by the anonymous device ID. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Deleting your data

You can erase everything at any time from Settings → Data → Reset all data. Uninstalling the app also removes the device ID and your local data. Because there is no account, deleting the app means the data can no longer be associated with you.

Children

DreamLog is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
